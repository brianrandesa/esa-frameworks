Ad structure

  • Hook: stop the scroll. Name the pain or the outcome in one line.
  • Body: agitate the pain, deliver the reframe or mechanism, drop one piece of proof.
  • CTA: one clear next step. Register for the free training, or book a call to see how we would fill your webinar.

About 70% of ads should be video, ideally talking head. Statics and carousels support.

Voice and hard rules

  • Confident, plain spoken, benefit led. No hype cliche, no fluff.
  • No dashes of any kind. No em dashes, no en dashes. Use commas, periods, or short sentences.
  • FTC safe. Never promise or imply specific earnings or results. No "you will make $X." Use illustrative ranges and call them examples.
  • Meta compliant. No banned claims, no before and after income, no personal attributes targeting language. Keep it clean.
  • Webinar focused and virtual first. We are not selling live in person events right now.
  • Buyers, not bodies. We fill webinars with buyers, not just attendees.

Reminder before you ship a script: no dashes, no earnings promises, one clear CTA, and it has to sound like a confident human, not a generic funnel.
