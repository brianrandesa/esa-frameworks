This Client Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] between Event Sales Agency, LLC ("ESA"), a [Florida] limited liability company, and [Client legal name] ("Client"). The parties agree:

1. Services

ESA will provide the one-time Webinar Launch build and the deliverables described in the attached Scope of Work (the "SOW"), which is incorporated by reference. ESA builds the system and fills the room; Client presents the webinar and makes all sales. ESA does not present the webinar or make Client's sales calls.

2. Fees

2.1 Build fee. A one-time fee of $15,000 for the Webinar Launch build and first webinar.

2.2 Performance fee. 10% of Back-End Revenue above $15,000. "Back-End Revenue" means gross amounts actually collected by Client from offers sold to attendees/leads of the webinar(s) ESA builds, during the engagement and for [90] days after each webinar. The 10% applies only to amounts above the first $15,000 collected. If Client does not recoup the $15,000, no performance fee is owed. Client will provide a sales report each month and ESA may reasonably verify it.

2.3 Additional webinars (optional). $5,000 each (full system + ad management).

2.4 Ad spend. Client funds its own ad account — $5,000 minimum per webinar — paid directly to the ad platform, not to ESA.

3. Payment Terms

The $15,000 build fee is due as: $7,500 on signing and $7,500 before the webinar launches (~30 days); or via approved financing (Affirm/Klarna). Performance fees are invoiced monthly, due within [10] days. Late amounts may pause work. The build fee is earned as work proceeds and is non-refundable once ESA begins the build, except as required by law or the financing provider's terms.

4. Timeline & Client Cooperation

Target launch is ~30–45 days from kickoff. The timeline depends on Client providing timely access (GHL, domain/DNS, ad account, checkout, calendar), assets, footage, and phase approvals. Client delays move the launch date day-for-day.

5. Intellectual Property

On full payment of the build fee, the funnels, automations, copy, slide deck, and creative built specifically for Client are Client's to use. ESA retains its pre-existing methodologies, templates, frameworks, checklists, and know-how, and may reuse them for other clients.

6. No Guarantee of Results

ESA does not guarantee any specific revenue, sales, registrations, attendance, or results. Outcomes depend on Client's offer, audience, pricing, presentation, and execution. Any figures, benchmarks, or examples discussed are illustrative industry standards, not a promise. Client is responsible for the truthfulness of its own claims, offers, and compliance with applicable law.

7. Compliance

Each party will comply with applicable law, including the TCPA, Telemarketing Sales Rule, CAN-SPAM, and Do-Not-Call requirements. Client is responsible for the lawfulness of its offer and any earnings or results claims Client makes to its own audience.

8. Confidentiality

Each party will keep the other's non-public information confidential and use it only to perform this Agreement.

9. Term & Termination

This Agreement begins on the date above and continues until the Webinar Launch is delivered (plus any optional additional webinars). Either party may terminate for the other's uncured material breach after [10] days' written notice. Fees earned through termination remain payable; Sections 2.2, 5, 6, 7, 8, 10–12 survive.

10. Limitation of Liability

Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential damages. ESA's total liability is capped at the build fee actually paid. Client funds and controls its own ad spend and is responsible for ad-account outcomes.

11. Independent Contractor

ESA is an independent contractor, not Client's employee, partner, or agent. This Agreement creates no partnership or joint venture.

12. General

Governed by the laws of the State of Florida; disputes resolved by mediation then binding arbitration in Miami-Dade County. Entire agreement; amendments in writing; electronic signatures accepted; severability; no assignment without consent.
