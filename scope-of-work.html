This Scope of Work describes everything ESA builds and delivers for the one-time Webinar Launch. It is incorporated into and governed by the ESA Client Services Agreement.

1 · Build & Funnel

  • Full virtual-event funnel built A→Z in GoHighLevel — registration, confirmation, reminder, replay & thank-you pages
  • CRM, pipeline, dispositions + tracking dashboard
  • A2P 10DLC registration + email/SMS deliverability setup (SPF/DKIM/DMARC)

2 · Automation & AI

  • Complete automation suite — confirmations, reminder sequence, no-show, replay, nurture, reactivation, post-purchase
  • AI Conversation Agent — speed-to-lead, nurture, qualification & follow-up
  • All SMS + email copy written and loaded

3 · Ads & Creative

  • Up to 50 static ad variations (client selects favorites) + the editable files
  • 20 video ad scripts (Hook / Body / CTA) + VSL script
  • Messaging + positioning + the hook that gets cold buyers to show up
  • Meta ad campaign setup; ESA runs the ads on the client's ad account

4 · Webinar & Sales Enablement

  • Full slide deck / pitch deck built for you (the proven 10/20-min structure)
  • The webinar framework + flow, and the sales script for your offer
  • High-ticket closing coaching + sales training + system walkthrough
  • Objection guide + follow-up cadence for non-buyers

5 · Delivery & Support

  • Day-of: one ESA team member on the call — all tech tested & run live, plus private coaching while you present
  • Weekly 1-on-1 build calls through the ~4–6 week build
  • Dedicated account manager + daily Slack access to the ESA team
  • 165-point ClickUp checklist with client view access + 8-phase QA — you approve each phase before we proceed
  • Video training for all 165 tasks + community (Skool) access — so you can run it yourself after

6 · Data & Optimization

  • Pipeline + tracking dashboard + the data
  • Industry-benchmark optimization calculator (registrants → show → book → close)
  • Post-webinar audit + a recommended plan for the next webinar

7 · What ESA Does NOT Do

  • Present the webinar — that's you (your expertise)
  • Make the back-end sales calls — that's you / your team

ESA fills the room and sets you up to convert. You present and you close.

8 · Client Responsibilities

  • Provide timely access: GHL, domain/DNS, Meta ad account, Stripe/checkout, calendar
  • Fund the ad account — $5,000 minimum ad spend per webinar
  • Provide brand assets, testimonials/case studies, and record raw footage on request
  • Approve each phase within the QA windows (delays move the launch date day-for-day)
  • Present the webinar and make the offer
